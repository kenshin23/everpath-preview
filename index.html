Meet the Founder
Founder Photo

Strategic Support.
Meaningful Outcomes.

Hi, I'm the Founder and CEO of EverPath Personalized Services, and an authorized Independent Support Broker in New York State.

My journey into Self-Direction began with seeing how complex and overwhelming disability support systems can feel for families. Navigating programs, understanding available resources, and making informed decisions often requires the right guidance, structure, and support.

Through EverPath, I provide families with clarity, organization, and strategic guidance throughout their Self-Direction journey. My goal is to help families better understand their options, navigate processes with confidence, and identify supports that align with their loved one's goals and needs.

With a background in Industrial Engineering and Project Management, I bring a structured, solutions-focused approach to a system that can often feel complicated and difficult to navigate.
